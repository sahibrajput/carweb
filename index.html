OUR SERVICES

TITLE HISTORY

Current and past state DMV title details are taken from the National Motor Vehicle Title Information System (NMVTIS).

ACTIVE & RECOVERED THEFT

Highlights instances when a vehicle has been reported as stolen, showing whether it is still missing or has been found by law enforcement.

WARRANTY DETAILS

Information about factory warranties, including coverage for the drivetrain and safety systems, as provided by the vehicle's manufacturer.

INSPECTION DATA

Federal highway inspection records provide information like when inspections happened, safety checks, problems with drivers, violations involving dangerous materials, and other details.

REGISTRATION DETAILS

This section provides the current registration information for the vehicle and lists any previously reported registration events, along with their respective dates.

VEHICLE VALUES

Valuation details from trusted sources give information about how much different types of vehicles, like cars and pickup trucks, are worth when trading them in or selling them. This includes values for selling them directly or through a dealer.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How Extensive Are Your VIN Reports??

Our reports include important information like Brand Records, Junk/Salvage/Total Loss status, Odometer Readings, Title Records, Prior Theft Records, and more. We give you detailed information to help you make good decisions!

Do You Cover All Vehicle Makes?

Our VIN checker works for all kinds of cars in the US, Europe, and Canada. You can search by VIN or use the license plate to find information about a vehicle.

What If I Can't Find Any Information?

Our proficient customer service team is available 24/7 to assist you in locating the information you need. Contact them at info@epicvincheck.com If, for any reason, our search experts cannot find your information, we offer a refund!

How Can I Cancel My Account?

How Many Reports Can I Generate?
